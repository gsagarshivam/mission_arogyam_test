About Mission Arogyam

Mission Arogyam is the dream project of our Institute BK Arogyam which is the most authentic name in the medical sector and was established 41 years ago in 1979. Mission Arogyam is developed with an aim of taking grassroots initiative for making positive changes in the lives of common people and helping them to be healthy by the means of pure Ayurveda. We aim to make people healthy with the rays of Ayurveda and eliminate Allopath.

Mission Arogyam provides wide range of services to doctors, patients, and common people. We are providing services and around all states of India and working with doctors, patients, educational institution and general public in order to provide them with permanent solution of all diseases and to prevent the diseases from reoccurring through Ayurveda. Mission Arogyam had concentrated on training in various sectors for awareness of importance of Ayurveda in our lives. Mission Arogyam believes in empowering its member with the opportunity to live their life healthy and on their own terms. With the motto of reading health and wealth, Mission Arogyam has continued to enrich the lives of everyone who is the part of the solution and those who believe in our products.
